Fried Chicken

Simple Fried Chicken Recipe

My version of fried chicken

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • garlic or garlic powder
  • pepper
  • salt
  • patis or fish sauce
  • all purpose flour and breading mix
  • cooking oil

How to Cook

  1. Mix the chicken with garlic or garlic powder, pepper, 1 tablespoon of patis. Marinate overnight.
  2. In a bowl, mix 1 cup all purpose flour with 1 cup breading mix. Add 2 teaspoon salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  4. Dredge the chicken in the breading mix and cook in the oil. Flip the chicken after 4 to 5 minutes or when golden brown.
