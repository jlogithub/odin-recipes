Adobo sa Asin

Fully cooked adobo sa asin

This version of adobo originates from Bicol and is very easy to cook and uses few ingredients

Ingredients

  • pork belly (preferred)
  • garlic
  • salt
  • vinegar
  • pepper
  • dahon ng laurel
  • water
  • oil

How to Cook

  1. Heat oil in a cooking pot and saute the garlic until medium brown.
  2. Add crushed pepper to infuse the flavor in the oil. Cook for 10 seconds.
  3. Add the pork belly and cook for 5-10 minutes. Keep stirring to avoid the meat sticking to the pot. You can use any pork cuts but I prefer belly part since we will render the fat.
  4. Adjust the heat to medium. Add 1 cup of water and 1 teaspoon salt and cover. Cook for 15 to 20 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  5. When the meat is tender, add more salt to your preferrence. Add the vinegar and dahon ng laurel. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Reduce the liquid and render the fat while stirring occasionally.
  7. Your adobo sa asin is now done and ready to serve.
